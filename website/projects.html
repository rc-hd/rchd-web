Our Signature Projects

Turning generosity into measurable impact — healthcare, clean water, livelihoods and life-changing surgeries that reach the people who need them most.

Healthcare • Since 2016

Guru Nanak Bhagwan Mahaveer Dialysis Centre

Saving lives and reducing financial burden for thousands of households across Secunderabad and Guntur. The cost of one dialysis session at GMC is only ₹300.

10,000+ procedures / year ₹3 Cr saved for beneficiaries / year Partners: Rotary Foundation, Bhagwan Mahaveer Relief Trust, Indian Red Cross
Healthcare • Since 2020

GMC Diagnostics Centre & Polyclinic

Affordable diagnostics, timely treatment and better lives — modern tests, scans and consultations at highly affordable rates, helping families seek medical help early.

75,000+ beneficiaries / year ₹12 Cr project
Water & Sanitation • Since 1998

Water & Toilets for Girls' Schools

A basic hygiene initiative bringing dignity to school girls across Hyderabad and surrounding regions, measurably reducing absenteeism.

30+ RO water plants installed 50+ toilets & handwash stations 10,000+ beneficiaries
Livelihoods

Women's Tailoring & Livelihood Program

Structured tailoring training at Jeedimetla and Dhoolpet (in association with SETWIN) — turning skill into dignity, income and self-belief.

Hundreds of women trained 2 training venues
Youth & Careers

Udyamini Program

Empowering young women for careers and leadership through mentoring, interview readiness and confidence-building. Piloted at Government Degree College for Women, Begumpet.

1,000 girls / year target 24 interactive sessions Sponsored by: Solitaire Group
Healthcare • Since 2019

Flow Cytometers for Childhood Leukaemia

Faster, precise diagnosis at Basavatarakam Indo-American Cancer Hospital and MNJ Cancer Hospital — giving hope to children and families facing cancer.

500+ patients / year ₹60 L saved for beneficiaries / year
Healthcare • Since 2023

CT Scan at Rotary Prolifics Diagnostic Centre

Critical imaging technology within reach for every family at the CR Foundation, Hyderabad — funded through a Rotary global grant, Rotary Club of Naperville and a Prolifics CSR grant.

2,000 CT scans / year 75,000 beneficiaries / year
Healthcare • Since 2018

Rotary Challa Blood Bank

Timely blood transfusions for children with thalassaemia and economically-challenged patients. A dedicated blood bank and mobile collection unit ensure safe, dependable supply.

3,000+ units / year 500+ units / month collected
Healthcare

Retina Centre

Safeguarding the greatest gift — sight. Specialised retina care and advanced eye surgeries made accessible at subsidised rates at Ramdevrao Hospital, Kukatpally, funded by the Golf for Charity Tournament.

Subsidised retina diagnosis & surgery Reconstructive surgeries: 150 carried out last year

Regular Community Service

Small Consistent Acts. Big Impact.

While major projects transform systems, regular community service transforms everyday lives — from blood-donation camps and blanket drives to school support, food distribution and visits to orphanages and old-age homes.

A snapshot of recent activities

  • Blood Donation & Free Health Checkup Camp — Institute of Chartered Accountants
  • Donation Drive at a Govt. School on Independence Day — 100 steel plates & 480 school bags
  • Donation drive at Friends Foundation Orphanage
  • Clothes for girl children — Women & Child Welfare Dept., Ameerpet
  • Grocery kits, cooked meals, PPE & sanitiser during the Covid-19 pandemic
  • Solitaire Interact community service — baked cookies for the underprivileged
  • Sweaters, blankets & a meal at Lourdes Old Age Home
  • Christmas goodies at the MNJ Cancer Hospital kids' ward
  • Blankets for students in Govt. hostels, Medak District
  • RO water plant at K. Thakellapadu Village
  • Live music & dinner for the specially-abled — Karunalaya Alphonso Home
  • Visit & fridge donation at Vijaya Home for Elders
