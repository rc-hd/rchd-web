Helping Hands of Rotary Trust

Utilising Every Rupee for the Greater Good

HHRT was created to ensure generosity reaches the ground efficiently, transparently and responsibly — directing contributions into carefully managed projects across healthcare, education, welfare and livelihoods.

Why donors trust HHRT

  • CSR & 80G ready — eligible for CSR partnerships and tax benefits under Section 80G
  • Strong compliance systems and transparent accounting
  • Clear reporting and complete documentation
  • Proven execution capability across major projects

During FY 2024–25, HHRT focused its efforts on three areas: healthcare (dialysis, diagnostics, CT-scan access and eye care), skill development (vocational programmes and educational support), and community infrastructure (sanitation for girls, safe drinking water and hygiene awareness).

Our Annual Fundraisers

Events That Turn Participation Into Impact

Bringing together Hyderabad's business leaders, professionals and families to fund the causes that change lives — where passion for sport and motoring meets philanthropy.

Golf for Charity Tournament

Since its inception in 2016, the Club's Golf Tournament has grown into one of the most prestigious events on Hyderabad's golfing calendar. Hosted at the Hyderabad Golf Association, it consistently attracts over 100 golfers a year — every swing a swing for service.

₹20 Cr+ total funds raised 1,000+ players over the years 100+ golfers each year

Projects enabled: Dialysis Centre at Secunderabad · Rotary Challa Blood Bank · Flow Cytometer at Basavatarakam Cancer Hospital · Girls' toilets across schools · Retina Surgery Centre at Ramdevrao Hospital · Medical Centre at CR Foundation.

Deccan Accelerates for Charity — TSD Rally

Full speed ahead, drive with a purpose. A Time-Speed-Distance precision navigation rally from Hyderabad to Dindi, the TSD format ensures a level playing field for all participants — where passion for motoring meets philanthropy.

Route: Hyderabad → Dindi Format: Time-Speed-Distance precision rally Current cause: Next-gen sequencing machine for MNJ Cancer Hospital

Every registration, sponsorship and partnership helps fund life-saving community projects — bringing personalised cancer diagnostics to underprivileged patients at no cost.
