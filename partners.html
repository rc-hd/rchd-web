Our Partners in Every Success Story

The Companies & Institutions Behind Our Work

Every dialysis session delivered, every child helped, every school improved and every village served has one thing in common: someone chose to care. We gratefully acknowledge the companies, families, philanthropists and institutions whose generosity powers our work.

Funding partners also include The Rotary Foundation (TRF) and international Rotary partners such as the Rotary Club of Naperville. As we continue our transformative efforts, we invite more change-makers to join our growing circle of impact.
